Spreadsheet vs. Real-World Servicing

Spreadsheets keep full floating-point precision internally and only display 2 decimals. Some servicing systems round to the penny at specific calculation boundaries, while others preserve higher internal precision and round only at presentation or reconciliation points. The important design decision is not whether rounding exists, but where it happens and which value becomes the source of truth.

Newton-Raphson Convergence

The 30/360 formula provides the initial guess. Newton-Raphson adjusts it for actual day counts, then a penny-level search finds the cent value that minimizes the last payment difference.
